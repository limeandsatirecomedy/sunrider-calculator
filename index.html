Your Information

Your Monthly Paid-As Title determines monthly commission eligibility. Career Title is your highest achieved title. Average ₹ per PV/QV can be edited to reflect your own estimate.

1. Retail Sales Bonus (Personal Sales Commission)₹0.00

Calculated as: Personal Volume (PV) × ₹38 × PV-tier percentage. The PV-tier rate is 0%, 3%, 5%, 8%, 10%, or 15%.

2. Fast Start Bonus₹0.00

One-time promotion bonus during the applicable Fast Start period. Sponsor match is shown separately when eligible.

3. Ace Royal Advancement Bonus₹0.00

₹25,000 per first-time Ace Royal advancement in your personal group, plus ₹25,000 matching amount when you are paid as Ace Royal or higher.

4. Unilevel Bonus₹0.00

First select your paid-as title above (Star or higher), then enter eligible PV at each level. Each level is calculated as: Level PV × ₹38 × level percentage.

5. Development Bonus₹0.00

Enter each qualifying parallel leg once, using the highest paid-as title in that leg. A qualifying leg contains an IBO paid as Ace or higher.

6. Monthly Fixed Bonus₹0.00

Ace Prime and above: multiply the plan amount for your title by the number of qualifying legs.

7. Check Match Bonus₹0.00

Hero+ can match qualified downline Unilevel and Turbo Infinity earnings. Enter amounts already filtered by the allowed generation depth.

8. Turbo Infinity Bonus₹0.00

Hero+ earns 0.5% on eligible sales from Level 6 onward. The company applies a 3% CV cap if necessary.

9. Icon Bonus₹0.00

First-time Icon bonuses are paid only while qualified at that Icon rank during the schedule.
